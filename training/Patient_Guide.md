## Welcome to Capital Lab

Capital Lab's patient portal gives you full control over your laboratory experience — book tests, track results, download reports, and monitor your health trends — all from your phone or computer.

**Portal address:** [your lab's URL, e.g., portal.labname.jo]

## 1. Getting Started — Login

### Create Your Account
1. Visit the patient portal
2. Click **"Register"**
3. Enter your full name, mobile number, and email
4. Create a password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Verify your mobile number with the SMS code

### Login
1. Click **"Login"**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

**Forgot your password?** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

## 2. Navigation — Your Dashboard

After logging in, you will see your **Patient Dashboard**:

- **Upcoming Appointments** — Your next scheduled visit

**Navigation menu:**

## 3. Booking a Test

### Step-by-Step Booking

**Step 1 — Select Tests**
- Use the search bar to find a test by name (e.g., "CBC", "Blood Sugar", "TSH")
- Or browse by category (Blood Tests, Hormones, Culture, etc.)
- Click **"Add to Order"** for each test you need
- You can also select a health **Package** (e.g., "Annual Checkup")

**Step 2 — Choose Your Branch**
- A map shows all branches with availability today
- Select the branch that is most convenient for you
- Opening hours and distance are shown

**Step 3 — Choose Date and Time**
- Select your preferred date from the calendar
- Available time slots are shown in green
- Select a time slot that works for you

**Step 4 — Patient Information**
- Confirm your personal details
- Select if this booking is for you or a **family member**
- Add your insurance details if applicable

**Step 5 — Summary**
- Review your order: tests selected, branch, date/time, estimated cost
- Click **"Confirm Booking"**

**Step 6 — Confirmation**
- You will receive a confirmation on screen
- A WhatsApp/SMS message is sent to your mobile
- The appointment appears in your **Appointments** section

### Home Collection
If you prefer a phlebotomist to come to your home:
1. Go to **"Book"** → Select **"Home Collection"**
2. Enter your address and preferred time window
3. Confirm the booking
4. You will receive a confirmation when a collector is assigned

## 4. Your Test Results

### Viewing Results
1. Click **"Results"** in the navigation
2. All your reports are listed by date
3. Click on any report to view the full results

### Understanding Your Results
- **Green values** — within normal range
- **Red/Yellow values** — outside the reference range (your doctor has been notified)
- Reference ranges are shown next to each test value

### Downloading Your PDF Report
1. Open any completed report
2. Click the **"Download PDF"** button
3. A professional PDF report is downloaded to your device
4. The PDF includes a QR code that anyone can scan to verify it is authentic

---

## 5. Health Tracker

The Health Tracker shows your key health indicators over time as charts.

### Available Trends
- Blood glucose / Fasting glucose
- HbA1c
- Hemoglobin
- Cholesterol (Total, HDL, LDL)
- TSH (Thyroid)
- Creatinine
- And more — any test you've had multiple times

### Using the Health Tracker
1. Click **"Health Tracker"** in the navigation
2. Select the indicator you want to see
3. Use the date range selector to view any time period
4. The chart shows your values over time with the normal range highlighted

---

## 6. Notifications

Your notification inbox shows all messages from the lab:
- Result ready notifications
- Appointment confirmations
- Appointment reminders
- Fasting reminders (for tests that require fasting)
- Doctor follow-up requests

**WhatsApp and SMS:** Most notifications are also sent to your mobile number automatically.

To mark a notification as read, click on it.

---

## 7. Your Profile

### Updating Personal Information
1. Click your name or **"Profile"** in the navigation
2. Update: name, date of birth, national ID, phone, address
3. Click **"Save"**

### Adding Family Members
You can book tests for family members under your account:
1. Go to **"Family Members"**
2. Click **"Add Family Member"**
3. Enter their name, relationship, and date of birth
4. When booking, select their name in Step 4

### Adding Insurance Information
1. Go to **"Profile"** → **"Insurance"**
2. Enter your insurance provider and policy number
3. This will be used automatically when booking covered tests

---

## 8. Best Practices

- **Fasting tests:** If your test requires fasting, avoid eating or drinking (except water) for 8–12 hours beforehand. You will receive a fasting reminder the night before.
- **Arrive 10 minutes early** for your appointment
- **Bring your national ID** or booking confirmation number
- **Check notifications** before visiting the lab — results are often ready the same day

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Cannot log in | Click "Forgot Password" to reset; check email for reset link |
| Did not receive SMS/WhatsApp | Verify your mobile number in Profile; check if number is correct |
| Cannot see my results | Results appear only after doctor approval; you will be notified |
| PDF download not working | Try a different browser; disable pop-up blockers |
| Booking slot not available | Try a different branch or a different time; call the lab to check |
| Wrong test listed | Contact the lab immediately — do not guess |
