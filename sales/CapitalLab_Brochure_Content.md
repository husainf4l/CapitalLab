# CAPITAL LAB
### The Complete Laboratory Management Platform

## Platform Overview

Capital Lab is an enterprise-grade, fully bilingual (Arabic/English) laboratory management system built for modern diagnostic centers in the Arab world. It replaces every paper form, spreadsheet, and disconnected system in your laboratory with one integrated platform — covering patients, lab staff, doctors, and management.

Deployed in the cloud or on your own server. Operational in 5 weeks. Paying for itself in under 6 months.

## Key Features at a Glance

| Module | What It Does |

## Patient Experience

1. **Book** — Patient searches for a test, selects a branch, picks a time slot — in under 2 minutes
2. **Arrive** — Receptionist confirms arrival; barcode label printed automatically
3. **Track** — Patient receives live status updates via WhatsApp/SMS
4. **Receive** — When results are ready and doctor-approved, patient is notified instantly
5. **View** — Full digital report with highlighted abnormal values — viewable on any device
6. **Download** — Professional branded PDF with QR code for instant verification

## Laboratory Experience

The lab dashboard provides a real-time view of every sample in your facility:

- Morning overview: pending samples, today's appointments, critical alerts
- Barcode scanning: scan any tube to get full patient/test context
- QC management: Westgard rules validated before results are released
- Result entry: manual or bulk import from analyzers
- One-click doctor notification when results need review
