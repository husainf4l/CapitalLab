# Capital Lab — Pricing Strategy
## v1.0 · 2026

---

## Overview

Capital Lab is offered in four commercial models. The recommended default for most clients is **Option B (Monthly SaaS)** for single-group labs and **Option C (Per Branch)** for multi-location networks.

All prices are in Jordanian Dinars (JD) and are exclusive of applicable taxes.

---

## Option A — Perpetual License Fee

**Best for:** Large institutions, hospitals, government labs, or clients who want to own the software outright.

### One-Time License Fees

| Package | Coverage | License Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Single Branch License | 1 branch, unlimited users | JD 12,000 |
| Multi-Branch License (up to 5) | Up to 5 branches | JD 25,000 |
| Enterprise License | Unlimited branches | JD 50,000 |

### Annual Maintenance & Support Fee (after Year 1)
- 20% of license fee/year
- Includes: all software updates, bug fixes, security patches
- Does NOT include new major modules (priced separately)

### Add-ons
| Add-on | Price |
|---|---|
| Source code escrow | JD 3,000 one-time |
| Custom module development | JD 150/hour |
| White-label branding | JD 5,000 one-time |
| Additional training sessions | JD 200/session |

**Pros:** No recurring fee. Full ownership. Predictable long-term cost.
**Cons:** Larger upfront investment. Responsibility for hosting and updates.

---

## Option B — Monthly SaaS Subscription

**Best for:** Single-branch independent labs, clinic-attached labs, startup diagnostic centers.
**Recommended for:** Most clients.

### Pricing Tiers

#### Starter — JD 400/month
- 1 branch
- Up to 10 system users
- Patient portal (booking, results, notifications)
- Lab dashboard (samples, barcodes, result entry)
- Basic billing (invoices, payments)
- PDF reports (basic template)
- Email + ticket support (24-hour response)
- 5 GB storage included

#### Professional — JD 900/month *(Most Popular)*
- Up to 3 branches
- Unlimited system users
- All Starter features, plus:
  - Doctor portal (review, approval, clinical notes)
  - Insurance module (claims, tracking)
  - Advanced billing (insurance, partial payments, aging)
  - Analyzer import (CSV, HL7, ASTM, XML)
  - Notification center (WhatsApp, SMS, Push)
  - Audit center
  - System settings
  - Executive analytics
  - Branded PDF reports (custom logo, colors)
  - Priority support (4-hour response)
  - Onboarding assistance included
  - 25 GB storage included

#### Enterprise — Custom Pricing
- Unlimited branches
- All Professional features, plus:
  - Custom integrations (HIS, government portals, insurance APIs)
  - On-premise deployment option
  - Dedicated account manager
  - SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
  - Custom branding / white-label
  - Custom reporting
  - Quarterly on-site support visits
  - 24/7 phone support
  - Unlimited storage

### Annual Subscription
- Pay annually: **20% discount** on all SaaS tiers
- Starter annual: JD 3,840/year (saves JD 960)
- Professional annual: JD 8,640/year (saves JD 2,160)

## Option C — Per Branch Pricing

**Best for:** Hospital groups, multi-branch networks, franchise lab brands expanding across Jordan.

### Per Branch Monthly Rate

| Branches | Per Branch/Month | Total Example |
|---|---|---|
| 1 branch | JD 700/branch | JD 700 |
| 2–5 branches | JD 600/branch | JD 1,200–3,000 |
| 6–10 branches | JD 500/branch | JD 3,000–5,000 |
| 11–20 branches | JD 400/branch | JD 4,400–8,000 |
| 21+ branches | JD 300/branch | Negotiate |

**Includes:** All features (equivalent to Professional) per branch.
**Discounts:** Volume pricing applied automatically at each tier.

---

## Option D — Enterprise / Government / Custom

**Best for:** Ministry of Health implementations, hospital chains, insurance company portals, academic medical centers.

### Engagement Types

**Government / MOH Deployment**
- One-time implementation fee: JD 30,000–80,000 depending on scope
- Annual license: JD 15,000–40,000
- Full source code delivery
- On-premise deployment
- Arabic documentation and training
- 5-year maintenance agreement available

**Hospital Group**
- Per-bed or per-sample pricing models available
- Integration with existing HIS (HL7 FHIR bridge)
- Custom patient portal under hospital brand
- Custom proposal on request

**Insurance Company Integration**
- API-first integration with existing insurance portal
- Lab portal white-labeled under insurance brand
- Per-transaction pricing available

## Setup Fees

Setup fees are separate from subscription/license fees and cover:

| Service | Fee |
|---|---|
| Standard setup (1–3 branches) | JD 800 |
| Multi-branch setup (4–10 branches) | JD 1,500 |
| Enterprise setup | JD 3,000+ |
| Data migration (from existing LIS) | JD 500–2,000 |
| Custom integration (per system) | JD 1,500–5,000 |

Setup includes: server configuration, deployment, master data entry assistance, and first training session.

## Hosting Options

|---|---|---|

## Commercial Terms

- Invoicing: Monthly (SaaS) or annual upfront (with 20% discount)
- Payment: Bank transfer; 14-day payment terms
- Contract term: Minimum 12 months for SaaS
- Cancellation: 30-day notice; data export provided within 7 business days
- Price increase: Max 10% annually, 60-day advance notice
- Pilot: 30-day paid pilot at Starter rate for new clients unsure of tier
